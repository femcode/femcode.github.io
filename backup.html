About Us


Our Mission

" FEMSCODE was created to help Counter The Culture in tech and teach girls to code, putting them on track to rewarding careers in Tech."

Our Vision

"To positively Enlighten and Impact Females and their interest in Computer Science and in the Tech world as a whole. "

Our Goal

"To reach out to all Female students of Yaba College Of Technology by providing them with the tools and curriculums to progress in Tech. "

Benefits

"Collaboration,reliable Mentors,free Personal Computers,free Internet Access,serene environment for learning. "

FemsCode

FemsCode also known as FemalesCode is a community that aims to Counter The Culture by building the Female Students Of Yaba College of Technology to become future Problem solvers and provide innovative solutions to problems through Tech, and in essence give females a voice in Tech.
